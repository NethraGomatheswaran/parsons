Responsive Poem

A reading experience for the poem, Bread and Cake written by Kevin Prufer is designed across three digital platforms. Keeping in mind the affordances and the contexts of these platforms, the experience is designed.

Art by Instruction

Inspired by Work from Instructions by Sol LeWitt, this piece allows people to create art from two, concise instructions provided to them. The medium of usage are custom squares of same geometric sizes and varying patterns.

Geometric compositions

Playing with colours and geometric shapes can result in interesting compositions. The goal is to learn to develop these patterns in the quickest and easiest possible ways using HTML & CSS tools.
